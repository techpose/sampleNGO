Your donation preserves heritage

Every rupee you contribute directly reaches rural artisans — ensuring their craft, their dignity, and their livelihood continue for generations.

Your Impact

What your donation does

🎨

Fund Skill Training

Your donation funds workshops where artisans learn to craft functional modern products using traditional techniques — expanding their market reach.

🌐

Enable Market Access

Help build the platform and logistics that connect artisans directly with customers across India and globally — eliminating exploitative middlemen.

🏺

Preserve Art Forms

Support documentation, training, and awareness for 13 endangered art forms at risk of disappearing. Your contribution is cultural preservation.

Tax Benefits

80G Tax Exemption

Gullakaari is registered as an NGO under Indian law. Donations to Gullakaari may be eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961 — subject to verification and your individual tax situation.

Registration: 12A Registered

For tax receipts and 80G certificates, please email your PAN and donation details to us after transferring.

Please consult a tax professional to confirm eligibility. This is not tax advice.
